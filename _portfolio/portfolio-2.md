paper_abstract: >-
  Landslide-mitigation barriers on steep, densely vegetated slopes are difficult and costly to inspect manually. We developed a LiDAR-based quadrotor with an integrated software system for robust localization, high-precision mapping, and navigation in cluttered under-canopy environments. The aircraft follows a pilot's high-level commands while autonomously avoiding small and dynamic obstacles. Field experiments conducted with the Hong Kong Civil Engineering and Development Department demonstrate safe maneuvering through dense vegetation and the system's practical potential for hazardous, hard-to-access slope inspections.
